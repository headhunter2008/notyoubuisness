List of user agreement

  • Terms of Service (ToS): A legal agreement between the service provider and the user, outlining the rules and guidelines for using the service.
  • End User License Agreement (EULA): A contract between the software application's licensor and the purchaser, granting the right to use the software.
  • Privacy Policy: A statement that explains how an organization collects, handles, and processes users' personal data.
  • Acceptable Use Policy (AUP): Defines what actions are and are not allowed when using a service, often pertaining to appropriate behavior, content, or usage.
  • Service Level Agreement (SLA): An agreement between a service provider and a customer that defines the level of service expected from the service provider.
  • Return and Refund Policy: Outlines the conditions under which a customer can return a purchased item and receive a refund.
  • Affiliate Agreement: An agreement between a company and its affiliates that sets out the terms and conditions for the partnership.
  • Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA): A legal contract that outlines confidential material, knowledge, or information that the parties wish to share with one another for certain purposes but restrict access to or by third parties.
  • Community Guidelines: Rules that outline the acceptable behaviors and content standards for users in an online community.
  • Copyright Policy: Outlines the rules and regulations related to the use of copyrighted material on a platform or service.
  • Code of Conduct: A set of rules outlining the social norms and rules and responsibilities of, or proper practices for, an individual, party, or organization.
  • Subscription Agreement: An agreement between a service provider and a customer that outlines the terms and conditions for subscribing to a service or product.
  • Employment Agreement: A contract between an employer and an employee that outlines the terms and conditions of employment.
  • Partnership Agreement: A contract between partners in a business venture that outlines the terms and conditions of their partnership.
  • Confidentiality Agreement: A legal contract that establishes a confidential relationship between parties, typically to protect any type of confidential and proprietary information or trade secrets.
  • Rental Agreement: A contract that outlines the terms and conditions of renting a property or equipment.
  • Lease Agreement: A legal contract outlining the terms under which one party agrees to rent property from another party.
  • Loan Agreement: A contract between a borrower and a lender that outlines the terms and conditions of a loan, including repayment terms and interest rates.
  • Consulting Agreement: A contract between a consultant and a client that outlines the terms and conditions of the consulting services to be provided.
  • Agency Agreement: A legal contract between two parties, one being the principal and the other being the agent, where the agent is authorized to represent the principal in certain transactions.
  • Vendor Agreement: A contract between a vendor and a client that outlines the terms and conditions of the goods or services to be provided by the vendor.
  • Franchise Agreement: A legal contract between a franchisor and a franchisee that outlines the terms and conditions of the franchise relationship.
